About Project

What is it?

Need

  • Delivery people are barred from entering the campus due to security issues.
  • Students want deliveries at their hostels rather than walking to the main gate after an exhausting day at college.
  • The canteens and shops inside the TIET campus do not have a centralised delivery system for the campus.
  • In case of a bad weather, it is difficult for TIET residents to go out and grab something from the COS complex, canteens or any other shop.
  • In todayâ€™s times of social distancing, contactless deliveries are of utmost importance.

    • Objective

    We are aiming to build an automated delivery transportation system, for usage inside the TIET campus. This product will be used to deliver food orders (Zomato, Swiggy etc), online orders (Flipkart, Amazon etc) from the main gate of the campus to the hostels.

    It may also be used to deliver other auxiliaries and food orders (COS complex and other canteens) within the campus.

    This delivery system will be synchronised with a mobile app for seamless and secure co-ordination.

  • Customer verification system.
  • Sensors to identify obstacles.
  • Theft-proof design.
  • Weatherproofing.
  • Stability of food carrying compartment
  • The speed of the vehicle should be able to suffice the orders.
  • Thermal insulation

    • May Haves :

  • A battery-powered, design.
  • Vending of soft drinks.
  • Solar-powered enabled.
  • Basic stationery vending.
